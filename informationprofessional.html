Information Professional
MANTRA is for you if you want to gain awareness of research data management practices and reflect on open access, licensing and publishing research data.

3 things you might want to use MANTRA for:
- Training support staff to increase awareness of institutional data management requirements.
- Assisting academics and research students preparing research data management plans.
- Being aware of the benefits of data sharing and licensing, and digital preservation practices.
