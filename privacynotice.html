MANTRA Privacy Notice

This privacy notice provides information about how the University of Edinburgh collects and uses your personal information in relation to MANTRA.

As a visitor to https://mantra.ed.ac.uk/ the only data we collect, store and use is your IP address.

We capture the IP address of every visitor to https://mantra.ed.ac.uk/. We do this so that we can monitor service user engagement.

The IP data is collected on our behalf by Google Analyticsa.

We will hold the IP data for three years.

If you have any questions, please contact the Research Data Support team or the service owner, Robin Rice, at https://www.ed.ac.uk/information-services/research-support/research-data-service/contact
